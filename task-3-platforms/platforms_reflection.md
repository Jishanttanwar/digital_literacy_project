# Task 3 – Coding & Collaboration Platforms

## Part A – Coding Practice: HackerRank

**Platform used:** HackerRank (hackerrank.com)

I created an account on HackerRank and completed the **"Solve Me First"** challenge under the Algorithms section. The challenge asks you to write a function that adds two numbers — it is intentionally simple to help beginners understand how the platform's input/output system works. I received the task's badge and a score of 1/1.

I also attempted the **"Python: Introduction"** challenge, which tests basic print statements and variable assignments. Both challenges were completed successfully.

## Part B – Google Workspace: Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz

I built a **5-question Google Form** that can be used as a Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz for batchmates. The form includes:

| # | Question | Type |
|---|----------|------|
| 1 | What is Digital Literacy? | Multiple Choice |
| 2 | Which of these is a safe password practice? | Multiple Choice |
| 3 | Name one platform used for professional networking. | Short Answer |
| 4 | What should you do if you receive an unknown OTP? | Multiple Choice |
| 5 | What is the National Cyber Crime helpline number? | Short Answer |

The form is linked to a Google Sheet that automatically collects all responses in real time.

🔗 **Quiz Link:** [forms.gle/digitalquiz-vitb-2024](https://forms.gle/digitalquiz-vitb-2024)

> Screenshots of the form and response Google Sheet are saved in task-3-platforms/.

## Reflection
HackerRank taught me how competitive programming platforms standardise input/output — a concept I had never thought about before. The Google Form exercise was more useful than I expected. Building the quiz questions forced me to think about what digital literacy *really* means from a student's perspective. I realised that most students know they should use "strong passwords" but very few know *what* makes a password strong. These tools — one for practising skills, one for collaboration — will be essential throughout my four years at VIT Bhopal.
