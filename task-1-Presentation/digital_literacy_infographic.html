What is Digital Literacy?

The ability to find, evaluate, create, and communicate information using digital technology.

It's not just about using a phone — it's about using it wisely.

Safe Internet Practices

  • Use strong, unique passwords
  • Enable two-factor authentication
  • Never share OTP with anyone
  • Check URLs before clicking links
  • Use VPN on public Wi-Fi
  • Report suspicious messages
👤

Professional Online Presence

  • Create a GitHub profile
  • Build your LinkedIn profile
  • Keep profiles updated yearly
  • Think before you post
  • Use your college email for signups
🛠️

Useful Digital Tools

  • Canva – Design infographics
  • Google Drive – Store & share files
  • HackerRank – Practice coding
  • Notion – Note-taking & planning
  • Grammarly – Writing assistant
📧

Email Etiquette

  • Clear, specific subject line
  • Address professor as "Dr." or "Prof."
  • Keep emails short & structured
  • Proofread before sending
  • Reply within 24 hours
  • Sign off with your full name + reg no.
⚠️

Cybercrime Warning Signs

  • "Send Rs. 1 to get Rs. 1000" → SCAM
  • Urgent OTP requests → NEVER share
  • Unverified QR codes → DANGER
  • Unknown job offers → VERIFY first
  • Report to: cybercrime.gov.in
  • Helpline: 1930 (24×7)
